About Me

After spending almost 10 years in the Events Marketing industry, I was, along with many others, left unemployed due to the effects of Covid-19 on the economy. I used this time to reflect and recalibrate on what I wanted to do with my career going forward. After making the decision to enroll in Thinkful's Full-Stack Developer Program, the prospect of being able to enter this field and work along some expert programmers is now a reality. Offering a full front to back end experience building out websites and apps to clients is something I look forward to being able to offer.

When I am not coding, exercising takes up my free time. In addition to chasing around a toddler, I am an avid runner and have completed to date 3 marathons and over 10 halfs. I also love to travel (you know, when that becomes a thing again). The excitement of traveling to new places and seeing new cultures is something that never gets old. Always having the desire to learn, both in life and professionally, is a driving force in my life. I am also a huge fan of Disney.
